Disney Movie Trivia: Classic & Modern Films

A movie-focused Disney trivia round that mixes classics, modern favorites, and the villains and sidekicks kids love.

If your family loves Disney movies, this trivia round is built for you. You’ll find a mix of classic films and modern Pixar favorites, plus a section dedicated to villains and sidekicks that kids remember instantly. Classic Disney Movie Trivia

1. In Cinderella, what does the Fairy Godmother turn into a carriage?

Answer: A pumpkin

2. Who is the villain in Sleeping Beauty?

Answer: Maleficent

3. In The Jungle Book, what kind of animal is Baloo?

Answer: A bear

4. In Peter Pan, who is afraid of the crocodile?

Answer: Captain Hook

5. In The Little Mermaid, what is Ariel’s human name after her wish?

Answer: She keeps the name Ariel

6. What is the name of the teacup in Beauty and the Beast?

Answer: Chip

7. In The Lion King, what is Simba’s mother’s name?

Answer: Sarabi

8. What animal does Pinocchio want to become a real version of?

Answer: A real boy (human)

Modern Disney & Pixar Trivia

1. In Frozen, what power does Elsa have?

Answer: Ice and snow magic

2. In Moana, who is the demigod who helps Moana?

Answer: Maui

3. In Turning Red, what animal does Mei transform into?

Answer: A red panda

4. In Toy Story, what does Andy call his dinosaur toy?

Answer: Rex

5. In Inside Out, which emotion is blue?

Answer: Sadness

6. In Brave, what is the name of Merida’s horse?

Answer: Angus

7. In Luca, where is the movie set?

Answer: Portorosso, Italy

8. In Encanto, which family member talks to animals?

Answer: Antonio

Villains & Sidekicks Trivia

1. Who is the villain in Aladdin?

Answer: Jafar

2. What is the name of Mulan’s cricket friend?

Answer: Cri-Kee

3. Who is Hercules’ sidekick trainer in Hercules?

Answer: Philoctetes (Phil)

4. In The Princess and the Frog, what is Tiana’s friend’s name?

Answer: Charlotte La Bouff

5. What is the name of the hyena trio in The Lion King?

Answer: Shenzi, Banzai, and Ed

6. In Tangled, what is the name of Rapunzel’s horse friend?

Answer: Maximus

7. What is the name of Stitch’s creator in Lilo & Stitch?

Answer: Dr. Jumba Jookiba

8. In Monsters, Inc., what is the name of Sulley’s best friend?

Answer: Mike Wazowski

How to Play Movie Trivia as a Family Game

Use movie trivia as a warm-up before a film, or make it the main event. Pick 12–15 questions, rotate turns, and let kids choose the order so they feel involved.

  • Play before movie night while snacks are ready.
  • Give a bonus point for singing a famous song lyric.
  • Let the winner choose the next movie.

Disney Movie Trivia FAQs

What counts as Disney movie trivia?

Disney movie trivia includes animated classics, modern Disney films, and Pixar movies. Many families mix them together in one round.

How do I balance classic and modern questions?

Try a 50/50 mix. Ask one classic question followed by one modern question so each generation has a turn to shine.

Are villains and sidekicks good trivia topics for kids?

Yes. Kids often remember sidekicks and villains because they are so distinctive, which makes those questions fun and approachable.

How many questions should I use for a movie trivia night?

A 15–20 question round keeps the pace lively while still covering several movies.
